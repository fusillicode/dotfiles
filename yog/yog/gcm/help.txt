Usage: gcm [install | init zsh | --pick | <branch-name>]

Interactive current-branch rename helper.

Modes:
  gcm                  Pick recent branch; leaves `gcm <branch>` in zsh input.
  gcm <branch-name>   Rename current branch to <branch-name>.
  gcm install         Add the one-line zsh wrapper setup to ~/.zshrc.
  gcm init zsh        Print the zsh wrapper.
  gcm --pick          Pick recent branch and print its name.
